Ingredients:
 - 100 grams flour
 - 25 grams baking powder
 - 75 ml sparkling water
 - 75 ml lager beer
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions:
 1. add the baking powder and flour into bowl
 2. slowly pour the mixed beer and water into the bowl (stirring as you mix)
 3. when smooth consistency reached leave to stand for 15 minutes

Note: cover the food you wish to batter with flour before drenching in the batter
        Ensure the fat is hot hot hot before adding battered food. Hint drop a spec of batter in to watch it crisp up. your are then ready to rock and roll.
